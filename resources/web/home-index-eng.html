Fagutvalgets kursevaluering

For students

At the end of every semester all IFI courses are evaluated. Invites to anonymous surveys are sent out to everyone registered for an IFI course that semester. An overview of your invites is available at nettskjema.uio.no. This system is developed by UiO (USIT) and guarantees anonymity of respondents.

The department of informatics (IFI) has given FUI permission to publis a summarized version of the course evaluation online for students. Comments regarding specific educators are not shown due to privacy concerns. Please note that course teacher get access to the full reports, with all comments.

For educators / administration

Access to the complete reports is available on a separate web site. If you do not have access to your course, e-mail fui-kk[æt]ifi.uio.no to gain access

Contribute

The course evaluation is an open-source project available on GitHub. If you have experience with python or web(js, html, css) there is a lot you can help with. See Issues for more information.
